From the transformed data the follow aggregations have been applied.

1. Two table for sales people with "attriton" and "non-attrition". Each table contains the SUM of the respective sales persons.

2. Three tables for all sales people, sales people with attriton, and sales people without attrition. Each table contains column for the following aggergations “average_monthly_income”, “min_monthly_income”, and “max_monthly_income”.

3. Two table for sales people with "attriton" and "non-attrition". With the aggergation of "Monthly income" and "Count".
